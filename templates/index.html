  L.A. County H1N1 "Swine Flu" Vaccination Calendar

Below is a list of clinics in Los Angeles County offering H1N1 vaccinations between Oct. 23 and Nov. 8, 2009. The vaccines are for high risk groups: pregnant women, people with kids younger than six month, healthcare workers, all people between six months and 24 years old, people between 25 and 64 years old who have health conditions that put them at higher risk. The list is drawn from a document published by the L.A. County Department of Public Health.

I've republished the list below in HTML because the government's site only provides the listing in PDF form. I've also provided the data as an Excel spreadsheet you can download by clicking here.

My opinion is that information of this importance should not be distributed exclusively via PDF, because PDFs don't do well in search engines and are more difficult to read on older computers and web browsers. I've written a longer rant on the topic here.

If you have any questions or suggestions, please forward them to me.
